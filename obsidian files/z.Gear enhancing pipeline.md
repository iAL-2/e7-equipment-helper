Rules
1. Flat stats are worse than % stat 99% of the time. As main stats, discard unless speed checking or in gear poverty(new player)
2. Flat stats is useful for stacking with % main stats, like attack scaling(aki) or hp stacking(any support/tank). defense scalers are more rare. points awarded to main stat with flat stat sub, and main flat stat with % stat sub
3. Sets are an integral part of rolling. Off set pieces are much less valuable. EG a hit set dps piece... very few heroes need both eff and damage, since they also need to be blazing fast.
